Short Bio


I am a Junior studying Computer Science at Missouri S&T. During my time here I have developed a passion for software development and engineering that pushes me to improve everyday. Currently, I practice web development both on a team and in my spare time working on both front-end and back-end. I strive to create attractive and flexible interfaces controlled by concise, efficient code. Someday, I hope to become a software engineer creating better tools for both users and developers.

Learning is something that I look forward to everyday. Whether in the classroom, with my peers, or in my free time I am always looking for ways to improve my ability to write cleaner, faster, more versatile code. My hope is that the future continues to bring me to those who motivate me to become the best I can be.

My life outside of my studies primarily includes spending time with friends and family, playing guitar, or unhealthily binge-watching TV shows.

Projects and Experience

ACM Member

Member of the Missouri S&T ACM organization. Actively contributing to ACM SIG-DOTCOM as a web developer for the ACM-General website. The website is responsible for keeping members up to date on current events, displaying information about our sponsors, officers, and SIGs, handling member dues, and much more! Throughout the project we have utilized several front and back-end technologies to ensure a responsive, reliable, and interactive website to fulfill the needs of the organization.

Web Developer for Art Glass Unlimited

Created a website for a local business owner to replace their outdated page. The front-end was developed using mobile-first, responsive techniques in HTML, CSS and JavaScript to ensure easy viewing on a variety of devices and browsers. The back-end uses Django to allow for quick updating of company media without the need for technical expertise.

ArchHacks 2017 Winner

Developed an Amazon Alexa skill to assist individuals with depression and anxiety through the use of personable voice prompts and the HADS scale. Winner of the Best Mental Health Hack and Best Data Visualization prizes.
